Vision 2030 Overview

Zimbabwe's comprehensive roadmap to becoming an upper middle-income economy by 2030, focused on inclusive growth, innovation, and resilience.

Vision & Mission

To transform Zimbabwe into a prosperous, empowered, upper-middle-income society by 2030.

Inclusive economic growth and structural transformation

Modern, world-class infrastructure development

Good governance and strengthening the rule of law

Environmental sustainability and climate resilience

Development Programs

Zimbabwe's strategic journey to Vision 2030 through three comprehensive development programs

TSP

2018-2020

Transitional Stabilisation Programme

Foundation phase focused on stabilizing the economy, restoring confidence, and establishing macroeconomic stability.

Key Achievements
  • Reduced inflation and fiscal deficit
  • Introduced currency reforms
  • Established foundation for NDS1
  • Fiscal consolidation initiatives
Status Completed

NDS1

2021-2025

National Development Strategy 1

Medium-term plan accelerating economic growth through structural reforms and infrastructure development.

Major Achievements
  • 6.3% average annual growth achieved
  • 820,000+ formal jobs created
  • Manufacturing now largest GDP contributor (15.3%)
  • Major infrastructure projects: dams, roads, energy
  • Record agricultural output in 2025
Status Final Year

NDS2

2026-2030

National Development Strategy 2

Final phase to achieve upper middle-income status and fully realize Vision 2030 goals.

Strategic Focus
  • Fiscal discipline and macro-stability
  • Create 2 million additional jobs
  • Halve poverty rate
  • Enhance property rights enforcement
  • Transition to mono-currency by 2030
Status In Planning

Transition to NDS2 (2026-2030)

Cabinet approved the NDS2 roadmap in September 2024. The 2026 National Budget will mark the first implementation year, building on NDS1's strong foundation of macroeconomic stabilization, infrastructure rehabilitation, and the structured ZiG currency system.

Infrastructure Investment Programme 2025

Consolidates achievements and provides foundation for NDS2 projects including Lake Gwayi-Tshangani (50% complete) and DISCO Steel Plant ($1.5B investment)

Inclusive Growth Framework

NDS2 will emphasize robust trade, investment promotion, and socio-economic transformation to catapult Zimbabwe to Vision 2030 targets
